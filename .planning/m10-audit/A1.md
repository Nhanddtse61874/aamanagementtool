### Summary
- **7 of 12** granular behaviors: COVERED.
- **5 of 12**: MISSING ‚Äî most significantly the **entire active-team switcher** (selection, persistence, and the cross-filter reset broadcast it drives) and **both integrity banners** (OneDrive conflict-copy, journal warning). These are not edge cases: a multi-team user has no way to change their working team on the web app today, and silent data-integrity conditions that used to surface to the user now only reach a server log (journal warning) or abort a background job invisibly (conflict-copy).
- No feature in this section is `CORE-SURVIVES` outright ‚Äî the closest is `SqliteMaintenance.FindConflictCopies`, whose *detection logic* lives in Core and survives, but whose *user-facing banner* (the actual behavior this inventory section documents) does not.
